Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

Welcome to Landify!

At Landify, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our platform to buy or sell land.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and location details.
  • Property Information: Details of land listings you create or inquire about.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate buying and selling of land through our platform.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, listings, or inquiries.
  • Improve and personalize your user experience.
  • Ensure site security and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • With trusted service providers who help operate our site.
  • With other users when you publish a land listing or send an inquiry.
  • If required by law, to comply with legal processes or protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions or concerns.

To request access or changes to your data, email us at: support@landify.com

6. Cookies

Landify may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.
